About Me

Software Engineer | New York, New York.

I was first exposed to coding in a C programming course in my freshman year of University. Afterward, I took every course I could that had anything to do with programming. I studied VHDL (a hardware description language for designing integrated circuits), as well as PLC for industrial digital computers and Assembly language for microcontrollers. By graduation, I realized how inclined I was to computer sciences and software engineering, which I am lucky enough to call both a passion and a career path.

In 2018, I moved to the United States and chose New York City as my home thanks to both the cultural and software engineering opportunities available. After getting my bearings and acclimating to the NYC dynamic, I was invited to attend App Academy’s highly selective, full-time–immersive Software Engineering Track, where I honed my skills in full-stack development, object-oriented programming (OOP), and Data Structures and Algorithms.
